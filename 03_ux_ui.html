03_UX/UI

Introduction

The 03_UX_UI project taught me a great deal about the process of creating a website. The focus was on using Figma as a tool to create initial concepts and prototypes without the need for coding. Through this project, I learned how to navigate Figmas interface and utilize its features to design layouts, user flows, and interactive prototypes.

The main objective was to build a user-friendly website based on a subject of our choice. For my project, I chose to combine two of my passions, football and fashion, and created a site that showcases content tailored to both themes.

Front page

Front page from my 03_UX/UI project.

Process

The process of this project involved a deep dive into research, idea development, and sketching. We focused on understanding the importance of each phase in the design process, starting with brainstorming and concept creation. One of the key aspects was learning to create wireframes and layout diagrams using Figma plugins, which allowed us to visualize the structure of our design early on.

Additionally, we explored how to develop a style tile, making critical visual decisions based on established design principles. As part of the process, we also created favicons and digital prototypes, allowing us to test and refine our designs before jumping into the coding phase.

Product

The final website design turned out to be a seamless integration of research, planning, and creative choices. Through the process of wireframing, layout diagrams, and using Figma plugins, I was able to structure the website in a way that was both functional and aesthetically pleasing. The design followed a clear path from the initial sketches to the final digital prototype, ensuring the site would be user-friendly and visually consistent.

The style tile I created helped guide the visual direction, from color schemes to typography, all based on fundamental design principles. Testing through prototypes allowed me to make adjustments early, ensuring the end result aligned with my vision before moving into the coding phase. The website ended up being a well-balanced combination of form and function, built on strong design foundations.
