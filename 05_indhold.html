05_CONTENT

PASSIONSITE

The 05_content theme was split into two projects, a passionsite and a redesign of a business site. The passion site introduced me to Adobe After Effects and Adobe Premiere Pro, where I edited an interview with a passionate jewelry maker.

I learned how to enhance storytelling through dynamic visuals and seamless editing, using After Effects for motion graphics and Premiere Pro for cutting and arranging footage. The experience deepened my appreciation for video editing and helped me express the subject's love for craftsmanship in a compelling, visual format.

Process

The process of creating this project involved learning to design animated vector graphics, working with LottieFiles for smooth animations, and enhancing the visual storytelling. I spent time mastering sound editing and video production, ensuring the audio complemented the visuals perfectly. Creating detailed storyboards helped me plan the sequence and flow of the content. This combination of skills allowed me to produce a cohesive, engaging project that effectively communicated the subjects passion, while also showcasing my growing technical abilities in animation and video editing.

Product

The product was a dynamic website designed to showcase a jewelry maker’s passion and craft. It featured a captivating hero section with a LottieFile animation that set the tone for the theme. The design incorporated thoughtfully chosen fonts and color schemes that complemented the jewelry-making theme, creating an elegant and cohesive visual experience. A key element was a video interview that brought the artisan’s story to life, providing an intimate connection with the audience. Additionally, a gallery was included to display the jewelry creations, offering a vibrant, interactive space for visitors to explore and engage with the artist's work.

BUSINESS SITE

The business site I worked on was a collaborative project with four other students, marking our first experience working together as a team. We used GitHub for version control, allowing us to simultaneously code in the same document and streamline our workflow.

The project involved redesigning the website for a real jewelry business, where we took inspiration from their existing online presence and integrated new content. We also captured fresh images inside the shop to give the website an authentic, personalized touch.

Front page

Front page from business redesign

Process

The process of creating the business site involved several key stages. We began by learning how to collaborate efficiently, coding together in the same file using GitHub, which was a new experience for our team. We started with a thorough design analysis of the existing website, identifying areas for improvement. Extensive research followed to understand the brand and user needs.

We then created wireframes and digital prototypes to map out the structure. Crafting compelling copy helped convey the brands message clearly. Layout diagrams guided our design decisions, and implementing CSS variables gave us flexibility and consistency across the site.

Product

The final product was a sleek, modern website designed to showcase the jewelry business in an elegant and user-friendly way. It featured detailed information about the business, its history, and the unique jewelry pieces on offer.

The layout was clean and professional, with easy navigation to key sections like product offerings, an about page, and customer testimonials. Contact options were prominently displayed, allowing potential clients to reach out easily. The website's refined design, paired with intuitive functionality, successfully captured the essence of the brand while providing a seamless user experience.
